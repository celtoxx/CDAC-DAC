Meta Platforms, Inc.,[14][15] formerly named Facebook, Inc., and TheFacebook, Inc.,[16] is an American multinational technology conglomerate based in Menlo Park, California. The company owns Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp, among other products and services.[17] Meta is one of the world's most valuable companies and among the ten largest publicly traded corporations in the United States.[18] It is considered one of the Big Five American information technology companies, alongside Alphabet (Google), Amazon, Apple, and Microsoft.

Meta's products and services include Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, and Quest 2. It has acquired Reality Labs, Mapillary, CTRL-Labs, Kustomer, and has a 9.99% stake in Jio Platforms.[19] In 2021, the company generated 97.5% of its revenue from the sale of advertising.[20]
